YITH WooCommerce Save For Later allows your customers to move items to a specific section that keeps them saved for a later purchase.

== Description ==

YITH WooCommerce Save for Later is a wonderful tool for your shop because it helps you with customers who have to leave your site without having purchased some selected products.
Put that they have added some items to their cart, but only when paying, they realise they have run out of money. Hours of search they have spent
on your site will be lost and probably they will never repeat hours of stressing search. So they will not buy... Yet, with YITH WooCommerce Save for Later, you can give them
a further opportunity and make them add these products to a specific section that keeps them saved even for months: so, each time they come to your shop, they will see the items
they have added right there reminding them of the purchase they wanted to do. And purchase it at their hand grasp, just with a click. That's great! Do you know how many more
items yu will be able to sell this way? Try it yourself!

**Main features:**

* You can move products from cart to your "Save for later" list
* You can change the name of "Save for later" link
* Use the shortcode provided to add "Save for later" section in your pages

For a more detailed list of options and features of the plugin, please read the [official documentation](https://yithemes.com/docs-plugins/yith-woocommerce-save-for-later/ "YITH WooCommerce Save For Later official documentation").

= Installation =

Once you have installed the plugin, you just need to activate it to enable it.

= Configuration =

YITH WooCommerce Save For Later will add a new submenu called "Save For Later" in "YIT Plugins" menu. From there, you can configure all plugin settings.
You will be able to add your "Save For Later" list in your pages thanks to a useful shortcode.

== Installation ==

1. Unzip the downloaded zip file.
2. Upload the plugin folder into the `wp-content/plugins/` directory of your WordPress site.
3. Activate `YITH WooCommerce Save For Later` from Plugins page
